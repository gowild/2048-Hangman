How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same image touch, they merge into one. Gradually the gallows are completed. Will hangman face his maker? You may be in for a surprise at the end.

Mobile version: Works great on your smartphone browser as well. Simply swipe up, down, left and right to move the tiles.
